## About Om Malik

Om Malik is a San Francisco-based writer, photographer, and investor. He has spent three decades in Silicon Valley as a journalist, entrepreneur, and venture capitalist. He founded GigaOM in 2001, turning a one-person technology blog into a pioneering media company. His writing connects technology, business, and its impact on humanity—with an emphasis on accessible, human language.

**Book:** *Broadbandits: Inside the $750 Billion Telecom Heist*
