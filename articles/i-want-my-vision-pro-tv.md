# I Want My Vision Pro(TV)!

**Source:** [On my Om](https://om.co/2024/01/08/vision-pro-is-coming/)  
**Date:** January 8, 2024  
**Category:** Technology

---

Apple has announced that its spatial computer, Vision Pro, is going on sale. Pre-orders for the $3,500 device, which ships on February 2nd. I am super excited. My excitement stems from my hands-on experiences with the device.

Apple touts Vision Pro as a new canvas for productivity and a new way to play games. Maybe, maybe not. Just as the Apple Watch is primarily a health-related device that also does other things, the primary function for Vision Pro is 'media'—especially how we consume it on the go.

In 2019, I wrote an essay about the future of television: I think both the big (TV) and biggest (movie theater) screens are going to go the way of the DVD. We could replace those with a singular, more personal screen—that will sit on our face. Virtual reality headsets are essentially the television and theaters of the future. Apple has made that headset.

Apple Vision Pro has ultra-high-resolution displays that deliver more pixels than a 4K TV for each eye. This gives you a screen that feels 100 feet wide with support for HDR content. The audio experience is just spectacular. For $3,500, you get a full-blown replacement for a reference-quality home theater, which would typically cost ten times as much. This is equivalent quality that you can take on the go.

We went from drive-in theaters to indoor theaters to multiplexes. On-demand on our screens is the future. I gave up traditional television screens when I started NewTeeVee in 2007. For me, my iPad is my TV. Vision Pro will extend that. There will be a lot of commentary about it being a disappointment. In time, we will come to realize that this was Steve Jobs' parting gift to the company he co-founded. He told Walter Isaacson: "I'd like to create an integrated television set that is completely easy to use. It will have the simplest user interface you could imagine. I finally cracked it."

I don't know about you, but I am excited and will be getting up early to order a unit for myself.

*January 8, 2024. San Francisco*
