# An End to Summer

**Source:** [On my Om](https://om.co/2025/09/01/an-end-to-summer/)  
**Date:** September 1, 2025  
**Category:** A Letter From Om, Technology

---

"Oh, it's a long, long while from May to December, but the days grow short when you reach September." — September Song

And just like that, "summer" as we know it is over. San Francisco has its own moods and seasons. We're kicking off our brief "Indian summer" that's going to last a couple of months.

### What Have I Been Up To?

To be honest, not much. I haven't been inspired and, as a result, not very creative. Over the years, I've learned that sometimes you can't fight whatever is preventing you from being creative. That lack of inspiration didn't prevent me from traveling. In late May, I took a trip to Svalbard with my friend Chris Michel. I created abstracts with my Leica Q3. In early July, I went to Santa Fe. Since then, I've stayed in San Francisco and enjoyed "Fogust." Pico Iyer: "Home is not necessarily the place that welcomes you. But it is the place where you stop thinking of other places."

### What's on my mind?

I've racked up serious time with various AI platforms. I continue to refer to AI (at present) as augmented intelligence. Everyone should try these platforms because they provide a clear understanding of where we are in the "hype cycle" and why this represents a powerful shift in how humans interact with information.

In summation, F. Scott Fitzgerald: "Life starts all over again when it gets crisp in the fall."

*September 1, 2025. San Francisco*
